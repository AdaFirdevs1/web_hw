Events

Contemporary Art and Artificial Intelligence

Under contemporary artist and educator Bager Akbay’s supervision, these seminars center on artists who work with contemporary technologies, how processes of creating art collections change, and the practices of creating digital art collections.

Library Talks

Join us for Library Talks, where we’ll explore the fascinating relationship between art and literature through a chosen book. The first book in the series is Margaret Atwood’s Cat’s Eye (1988), which offers a rich portrait of a young woman artist and her creative journey.

Expectations and Encounters

Swiss landscape architect Günther Vogt discusses perceptions of landscape from Switzerland and other geographies in the event titled "Expectations and Encounters." After its debut in Istanbul, the talk will also be held in Ankara, exploring the transformation of landscape imagery over time alongside reflections on national identity and the cultural production of space.
