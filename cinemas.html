The Foreigners of the Oscars

This exciting category, which showcases the highlights of world cinema, stands out every year as one of the most competitive categories of the Oscars. Representing different countries and cultures, these films reflect the alternative face of the Oscars and are made up of films coming from outside of Hollywood that have been screened and won awards at various festivals around the world.

Count Us In!

With the participation of directors and cast, the program creates a festival-like atmosphere, showcasing works that range from documentary to fiction. These films offer fresh ideas and new perspectives to storytelling and visual style. From women’s issues to social struggles and human stories from across Türkiye, the selection highlights films that often don’t get the attention they deserve in mainstream theaters.
